WORSHIP BAND — Audio Files
==========================

Drop your MP3 files here with these exact names:

  fire-on-the-altar.mp3       (Up-tempo shouting music)
  victory-in-jesus.mp3        (Classic southern gospel)
  holy-ghost-power.mp3        (High energy organ shout)
  down-by-the-riverside.mp3   (Swing gospel, clap-along)
  precious-lord.mp3           (Slow altar call testimony)
  i-got-the-joy.mp3           (Fast handclap gospel)

If a file is present, the game will play it with real audio.
If not, it will use the synthesized organ version.

You can also upload MP3s directly from the in-game song picker
using the upload button next to each track.

Supported formats: MP3, OGG, WAV, M4A
